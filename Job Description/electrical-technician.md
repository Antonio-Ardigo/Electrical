# Job Description — Electrical Technician (Site Survey & Data Capture)

**Engagement:** WSM Electrical **Single Point of Failure (SPOF) Assessment** —
21 assessment units, Central & Eastern sector (Riyadh, Madinah, Qassim, Eastern
Region) groundwater & surface-water desalination plants.
**Reference:** WSM Subcontractor Technical Specification `WSM-SUBC-SPEC-001 Rev 0`
([in this folder](WSM%20Subcontractor%20Technical%20Specification%20Rev%200.docx)).

> **Note:** The specification's fixed headcount (§3.1) is **1 PM + 2 Electrical
> Engineers**. This technician profile is an **optional field-support role** to
> strengthen the **non-intrusive site surveys and data capture** (spec §3.3) and the
> SF-01 document/asset inventory; include only if WSM's scope/pricing allows.

| | |
|---|---|

## Role summary

Provide hands-on field support to the assessment engineers during the **non-intrusive
site surveys**: capture as-found electrical data, support the asset/document inventory
and the verification of single-line diagrams, all under the engineers' direction and
within host-plant QHSE and permit-to-work rules.

## Key responsibilities

- **Site survey support** — accompany the engineers on **non-intrusive** field surveys
  at the host plants; help locate and identify MV/LV equipment against the drawings.
- **Asset & document inventory (SF-01)** — record equipment nameplate data (ratings,
  make/model, tags) for switchgear, transformers, LV switchboards/PCC, MCCs, VFDs,
  PFC, generators, ATS, UPS, batteries/chargers and earthing.
- **SLD verification support** — assist in **red-lining single-line diagrams** against
  the as-found installation; note discrepancies for the engineers.
- **Data capture** — photographs, panel/label readings, switch/breaker status and
  configuration, accessible meter readings; organize survey data per plant.
- **Condition observations** — note visible condition, housekeeping, thermographic
  hot-spots (where permitted) and obvious reliability concerns (e.g. duty/standby on a
  shared board) for engineer follow-up.
- **HSE & data** — strictly follow host **permit-to-work**, LOTO and QHSE
  (ISO 45001) rules; **non-intrusive only**; comply with the **NDA** and **NCA
  cybersecurity (v6.0)** data-handling requirements (no retention/disclosure of client
  data).

## Minimum qualifications

- Electrical **technician/trade qualification** (diploma / equivalent) and a valid
  electrician's license where required.
- 3+ years in industrial electrical installations; **LV competence**, **MV awareness**.
- Comfortable **reading single-line and wiring diagrams** and identifying equipment by
  tag.
- Familiar with electrical **survey/test instruments** (multimeter, clamp meter,
  insulation/earth tester, IR thermography) for non-intrusive use.
- **Arabic & English** working proficiency; valid driving licence (multi-site travel).

## Skills & competencies

- Accurate field data capture and record-keeping; methodical and safety-first.
- Awareness of relevant **IEC / IEEE / NFPA 70E** requirements at a technician level.
- Team player able to work to engineers' instructions across multiple plant visits.

## Key deliverables

- Per-plant **survey data packs** (nameplate inventory, photos, configuration notes).
- Red-line mark-up support for the SLD verification.
- Clean, organized data handed to the engineers for SF-01…SF-04 and the final report.

*Optional role aligned to WSM-SUBC-SPEC-001 Rev 0; inclusion, scope and rate subject
to WSM agreement.*
